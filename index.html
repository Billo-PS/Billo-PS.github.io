Legends: Arceus Proofing:

Due to the complexity of the Effort Level system, only full 31 IVs are counted towards the IV proof barrier.

Overworld & Outbreak Encounters:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture, leading into in-box Effort Level evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature of the Pokémon.
  • Your Pokémon must be sent to the party. Ensure you have a slot available for it to go to. This is due to the compexity of the box system.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved by RNG abuse, you should include screenshots of tools used to further prove IVs. You may also use overlays such as capturesight accompanied with a proof video to prove the IVs.
  • Disable autosave to re-encounter most Pokémon in the overworld. Use this to your advantage and save upon encountering any Pokémon you believe may need proof, and you can then adjust your party accordingly.

Scripted, Gift & Legendary Encounters:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture or redemption leading into in-box Effort Level evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature of the Pokémon.
  • Your Pokémon must be sent to the party. Ensure you have a slot available for it to go to. This is due to the compexity of the box system.
  • Some scripted/legendary encounters have an involved cut-scene, in the event of this you may start your video recording from any point during the cut-scene.
  • Tips, tricks and advice for proofing scripted/legendary encounters can be found in the example directly below.
